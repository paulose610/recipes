Pina Colada

Velvety-smooth texture and a taste of the tropics are what this tropical drink delivers. The easy-to-make Pina Colada recipe can be mixed and chilled ahead of time. When ready to serve, just blend for a creamy and delicious beverage.

Ingredients:

  • Pineapple juice
  • Coconut Cream
  • Rum

Steps:

  1. In a 2-qt. pitcher:
    1. combine 2-1/4 cups pineapple juice
    2. add 1 can cream of coconut
    3. add 1-1/2 cups rum
  2. Chill
  3. Add 2 teaspoons of lemon juice
  4. For each serving, place a generous cup of rum mixture and 1 cup ice in a blender
  5. Cover and process until smooth
  6. Pour into a chilled hurricane glass
  7. Garnish with a pineapple wedge
