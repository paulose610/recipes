French 75

French 75 was a drink created at Harry’s New York Bar, which is located in Paris France, during WWI. The original version of the drink had a powerful kick, like French 75 mm artillery gun. The version here uses less gin.

Ingredients:

  • gin
  • confectioners' sugar
  • lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker three-fourths full with ice
  2. Add 3/4 of an ounce of gin, 1 tablespoon of confectioners’ sugar
  3. Add 2 teaspoons of lemon juice
  4. Cover and shake 10-15 seconds or until condensation forms on the outside of the shaker
  5. Strain into a flute glass and top with champagne
